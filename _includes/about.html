About Me

Hi, I’m Raf—short for Rafael!

I’m originally from Portugal but have been living in Bristol for nearly nine years now (time really flies!).

I’ve been working in the fitness industry since 2011, starting in Portugal and continuing here in Bristol. Most of my career has been focused on coaching small group classes, particularly as a CrossFit Coach. In early 2024, I earned my CF Level 3 qualification.

I have a bachelor’s degree in Education and Physical Activity and have had the opportunity to work with a wide range of individuals, including:

  • Teens (11+)
  • Adults
  • Pre- and postnatal clients
  • Seniors with varying levels of mobility and independence

I’m passionate about helping people move better, feel stronger, and enjoy their training. Looking forward to working with you!
